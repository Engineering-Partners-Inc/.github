# Engineering Partners Inc.

Engineering Partners Inc. (EPI) is a women-owned engineering consulting firm specializing in **utility infrastructure** and **industrial power system** projects across the United States.

We partner with utilities, municipalities, and industrial clients to design, modernize, and harden electric systems that support safe, reliable, and resilient power delivery.

---

- **Electric Distribution Engineering & Design**
